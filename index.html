Lethbridge Alberta, Canada

Nestled along the banks of the Oldman River in southern Alberta, the city of Lethbridge serves as a gateway between the wide prairie and the rising Rockies. Founded in the early 1890s on coal mining roots and later becoming an agricultural and educational hub, it now counts more than 100,000 residents. With over 320 days of sunshine each year and a semi-arid climate shaped by Chinook winds, the city offers broad skies, brisk winds and dramatic light. Walking through its coulees, green-belt trails and the iconic High Level Bridge, you sense both a legacy of industry and a growing spirit of innovation. Whether you come seeking the calm of wide-open horizons or the promise of growth and community, Lethbridge stands at the crossroads of past and possibility.
